Micro-Interactions

Transitions-This is when an image moves or changes with the screen as the user scrolls.

Moving Images- This is when a user's curser moves over an image causing it to move or change without clicking or selecting the image.

Movement

Glitch movement is a growing design trend when a gif or video appears to have glitches in the screen.

Glitch Movment is not right for every webpage consider these steps before adding glitch movment.

  1. Consider the overall message of the wepage.
  2. Does this style coresponded with this new digital media?
  3. Choose the media to have glitch movement: video, images, and text.
